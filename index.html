KeekyForTheWeekend

There is so much to do while you are in Chicago for a weekend! From iconic landmarks like Millennium Park and the Skydeck at Willis Tower to world-class museums such as The Art Institute and the Field Museum, you'll find endless ways to explore the city's rich culture and history. Whether you're strolling down the Magnificent Mile for some shopping, savoring deep-dish pizza at a local favorite, or enjoying the vibrant nightlife in neighborhoods like River North or Wicker Park, Chicago offers something for everyone. I've gone ahead and compiled a list of my favorite places to go, enjoy!

Favorite Cocktail Bars

  • Toon's Bar and Grill
  • RAISED
  • Bird's Nest

Favorite Restaurants

  • Spacca Napoli
  • Coda di Volpe
  • Wakamono

Chicago's food scene is a melting pot of diverse flavors and experiences. From iconic deep-dish pizza to innovative global cuisine, the city offers something for every palate. Whether you're indulging in the artisanal dishes at Spacca Napoli, savoring southern Italian flavors at Coda di Volpe, or enjoying fresh sushi at Wakamono, you're guaranteed an unforgettable dining experience. The food isn't just a meal—it's a reflection of Chicago's rich cultural tapestry. Pair your meal with a night out at one of the city’s top cocktail bars like Toon's Bar and Grill, the rooftop vibes of RAISED, or the laid-back charm of Bird's Nest. Every bite and sip will give you a taste of what makes Chicago a world-class food destination.

Favorite Parks

  • Maggie Daley Park
  • Grant Park
  • Oz Park

Favorite Concert Venues

  • The Metro
  • Salt Shed
  • Northerly Island

If you're looking for the perfect blend of nature and entertainment, Chicago has it all. Spend a sunny afternoon in the lush greens of Maggie Daley Park, wander through the historic Grant Park, or enjoy a stroll in Oz Park, where the city’s urban landscape meets natural beauty. When the sun sets, Chicago comes alive with music. Catch unforgettable live performances at iconic venues like The Metro, experience the energy of outdoor shows at Northerly Island, or soak in the vibes of Chicago’s newest hotspot, Salt Shed. Whether you're lounging in a park or dancing at a concert, Chicago offers the perfect setting for every mood.
